## HMW TEMPLATE

**HMW #X: [Question starting with "How might we..."]**

**Objective Link:**
- Which $name business objective(s) does this address?
- If it addresses multiple objectives, list all

**Concrete Action:**
- Specific 90-day project with defined deliverables
- Not a vague initiative - should be executable pilot or POC
- Include scope: team size, timeline, budget range

**Red Hat Technology:**
- Primary Red Hat product(s)
- Supporting tools or integrations

**Quick Win or Strategic:**
- QW (Quick Win <6mo): Demonstrates value in current fiscal year
- ST (Strategic 6-18mo): Requires multi-phase rollout and organizational change

**Success Criteria:**
- How will we measure if the HMW solution worked?
- Must be quantitative and observable

---

## QUALITY CRITERIA

✅ **SPECIFIC:**
- Good: "How might we reduce containerized app deployment time from 2 weeks to 2 days?"
- Bad: "How might we improve efficiency?"

✅ **MEASURABLE:**
- Good: "How might we automate 80% of compliance reporting to reduce audit prep from 200 hours to 40 hours?"
- Bad: "How might we make compliance easier?"

✅ **ACTIONABLE:**
- Good: "How might we migrate the customer portal API from monolithic WebSphere to OpenShift microservices to support 10x traffic growth?"
- Bad: "How might we modernize our applications?"

✅ **TIME-BOUND:**
- Include baseline (current state) and target (desired state)
- Specify measurable outcome

---

## DIVERSITY REQUIREMENTS

Your 8 HMWs must include:
- **At least 2 focused on cost reduction:** Direct operational savings or efficiency gains
- **At least 2 focused on revenue growth:** Faster time to market, new digital channels, customer experience
- **At least 1 focused on risk mitigation:** Security, compliance, resilience, downtime reduction
- **At least 1 focused on innovation/competitive advantage:** AI/ML, edge computing, data-driven insights

## EXAMPLE HMW

**HMW #1: How might we reduce RHEL patching time from 40 hours/month to 4 hours/month while maintaining 100% compliance?**

**Objective Link:**
- "Reduce IT operational costs by $5M annually" (Objective #2)
- "Achieve SOX compliance with zero audit findings" (Objective #3)

**Concrete Action:**
- 90-Day Pilot: Deploy Ansible Automation Platform to automate patch management for 100 development servers
- Team: 2 infrastructure engineers + 1 Red Hat consultant
- Budget: $50K (Ansible licenses + consulting)
- Deliverables:
  * Automated playbooks for RHEL 8/9 patching
  * Integration with Red Hat Satellite for patch content
  * Compliance reporting dashboard showing patch status
  * Runbook for production rollout

**Red Hat Technology:**
- Primary: Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform 2.4
- Supporting: Red Hat Satellite 6.15, Red Hat Insights

**Quick Win or Strategic:**
- **QW (Quick Win):** ROI measurable in 4 months
  * Time savings: 36 hours/month × $150/hr = $5,400/month = $64,800 annual
  * Payback period: 3 months
  * Success enables broader automation initiatives (strategic follow-on)

**Success Criteria:**
- Patch deployment time: ≤4 hours for 100 servers (baseline: 40 hours)
- Zero failed patches requiring manual rollback
- 100% compliance reporting automation (baseline: 60% manual effort)
- Infrastructure team satisfaction score ≥8/10 on usability survey

---

**HMW #2: How might we enable the data science team to deploy ML models to production in days instead of months?**

**Objective Link:**
- "Accelerate AI-driven personalization to increase customer conversion by 15%" (Objective #1)

**Concrete Action:**
- 90-Day Pilot: Deploy OpenShift AI platform for model training and inference
- Scope: Migrate 1 existing ML model (product recommendation engine) to OpenShift
- Team: 3 data scientists + 1 MLOps engineer + 1 Red Hat AI specialist
- Budget: $120K (OpenShift AI licenses + GPU nodes + implementation)
- Deliverables:
  * OpenShift AI cluster with Jupyter notebooks and model serving
  * CI/CD pipeline for model versioning and deployment
  * Inference API integrated with e-commerce frontend
  * Monitoring dashboard for model performance and drift

**Red Hat Technology:**
- Primary: Red Hat OpenShift AI (formerly OpenShift Data Science)
- Supporting: OpenShift Container Platform 4.15, OpenShift Pipelines (Tekton)

**Quick Win or Strategic:**
- **ST (Strategic 6-9 months):**
  * 90-day pilot proves concept
  * Months 4-6: Migrate 3 additional ML models
  * Months 7-9: Full production rollout for all data science workflows

**Success Criteria:**
- Model deployment time: ≤3 days (baseline: 6-8 weeks)
- Data scientist productivity: 5 model experiments/week (baseline: 1-2/month)
- Production model uptime: ≥99.9%
- Business impact: 15% increase in customer conversion measured via A/B testing
