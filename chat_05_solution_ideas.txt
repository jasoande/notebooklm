## EXAMPLE IDEA

**Idea #1: Automate RHEL patching and compliance reporting across 1,200 production servers**

**Objective Alignment:**
- "Reduce IT operational costs by $5M annually" (Business Objective #2)
- "Achieve SOX compliance with 100% audit pass rate" (Business Objective #3)

**Success Metric:**
- Reduce monthly patching time from 40 hours to 4 hours (90% reduction)
- Achieve 100% compliance reporting automation (currently 60% manual)
- Zero unplanned downtime due to patching errors (currently 2-3 incidents/year)

**Red Hat Technology Stack:**
- Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform 2.4 (Automation Controller + Automation Hub)
- Red Hat Satellite 6.15 for patch management and content distribution
- Red Hat Insights for predictive analytics and drift detection

**Implementation Partner:**
- Accenture Federal Services (AFC): Red Hat Premier Partner with proven track record in large-scale RHEL automation for $industry customers. AFC implemented similar solution for [Comparable Company] with 3,000+ servers.

**Feasibility & Timeline:**
- **Quick Win (<6 months)**
  * Phase 1 (Months 1-2): Pilot with 100 dev/test servers
  * Phase 2 (Months 3-4): Expand to 500 non-critical production servers
  * Phase 3 (Months 5-6): Full production rollout across 1,200 servers

**Estimated Value:**
- **Financial:** $180K annual savings (45 hours/month @ $150/hr blended rate × 12 months)
- **Operational:** Free up 2 FTEs currently dedicated to manual patching, redeploy to cloud migration project
- **Risk:** Eliminate compliance violations ($50K-$200K potential fines), avoid downtime costs ($500K per incident)

**Prerequisites:**
- CIO approval and budget allocation ($250K for Ansible + Satellite subscriptions, $150K for implementation services)
- Infrastructure team trained on Ansible best practices (3-day Red Hat training for 5 engineers)
- Change management process updated to allow automated patching in maintenance windows
